SBHS Robotics Parent Association

The SBHS Robotics Parent Association is a non-profit group comprised of parents of current and former Robotics students. The Parent Assiciation is committed to ensuring that all of our teams have the resources they need to be successful in competition and otherwise. VEX Robotics is a very community-oriented by nature, and cooperation between our teams, our teachers, and our parents is essential to meeting our high standards.
