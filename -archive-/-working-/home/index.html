Congratulations to teams C and E!

Congratulations Teams C and E for their astounding performance at BCIT. C and E allied for finals and came home as tournament champions! Team E also won the Excellence trophy, and our teams are preparing to move on to the next level of competition.


Our Home Competition

Our competition at SBHS was a huge success, and we are proud to have hosted the largest VRC event in New Jersey history with 51 teams! Thank you to everyone who helped make the weekend possible, especially to our student and parent volunteers.
