VRC is a student-centered program
Per the rules and spirit of the VEX Robotics Competition, we expect you as a student to take initiative and assume responsibility, as well as to pass along information to your parents/guardians. Attention to detail, asking questions, and effective communication are universally useful practices that are important to us, and will serve you well in your career.

Questions/Concerns
You can reach out to us at sbhsrobotics750@gmail.com with any questions/concerns. If you are unsure about something or have extenuating circumstances, communication is key. We want to work with you and be as accommodating as possible while still being fair to everyone - but we can only do that if you communicate with us in a timely manner.
